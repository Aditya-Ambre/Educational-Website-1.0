Our Story . . . . . . .

TUTIX is a Social Networking Platform that connects students from all around the globe.

The platform is designed especially for STEM students at the undergraduate level. With the help of TUTIX, you can -

  • Join, search & Create your own small permanent groups for a course
  • Search and connect with numerous small groups of students temporarily.
  • You can call or text a group member.
  • Learn and Help others using our Visualization features.
  • Connect with a student permanently as a friend
  • Get Rewards & benefits as and when you help others
  • Translate the texts and explanations to your preferred language.
